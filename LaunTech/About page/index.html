LAUNTECH

LaunTech is a student organization that aims to improve entrepreneurial skills
in the engineering section by offering a suitable environment
to glow up your idea in a competition.

OUR MAIN AIMS

-Create and develop an entrepreneurial environment in engineering and (CS) facilities in Egypt.
-Create an annual hackathon where students can meet and interact with real investors.
-Develop a technical season to create real life tech applications that solves the Egypt grand challenge generally,
and Helwan university's challenge specifically.
